Services

01.

  New Build

At our firm, we specialize in designing new construction projects with a team experienced in single-family homes to multi-unit residential. We work closely with clients and contractors to ensure a smooth process from start to finish, offering comprehensive design services including space planning, material selection, color coordination, and lighting design. Our goal is to create beautiful and timeless functional spaces that reflect our clients' personal style and preferences, using the latest design innovations and technology while adapting to different project scopes, timelines, and budgets.

02.

  Renovation

Our firm specializes in transforming outdated or worn-out spaces into beautiful and functional ones. Our renovation section demonstrate the impact of a well-planned renovation. We work closely with clients to ensure their vision is incorporated into the final design, providing expert advice on materials and finishes while keeping budget and timeline in mind. Our attention to detail and commitment to quality workmanship ensures stunning results, whether you're updating a kitchen, revamping a bathroom, or giving your entire home a makeover.

03.

  Office Design

We believe that well-designed offices can improve productivity and boost employee morale. Our experienced team specializes in creating functional and aesthetically pleasing workspaces tailored to each client's unique needs and goals. Our services include space planning, furniture selection, lighting design, and branding. GET TO KNOW US

Our Team

Ar. Saili Mestry

Founder

Saili Mestry is a renowned interior designer known for her creative vision, commitment to safety, and aesthetic excellence. With seven years of experience, she has crafted stunning interiors for residential homes and commercial workplaces. Saili's designs prioritize optimal space utilization, incorporating Vastu principles and environmental elements for enhanced aesthetics and energy efficiency. She stays up to date with the latest design trends and utilizes sustainable materials to minimize environmental impact and operating costs. Saili holds a Bachelor's degree in architecture from the University of Mumbai. Her expertise in blueprint verification and construction feasibility adds depth to her designs. With a passion for sustainable design, Saili continues to expand her knowledge and believes in its potential to address global challenges. Her practical approach and inspiring designs have garnered praise from numerous satisfied clients. Contact Saili Mestry to bring your design vision to life.

Id. Pari Patil

Founder

Pari Patil is an award-winning interior designer with several years of experience in the field. She has a strong academic background, having completed her Bachelor's degree in Interior Design from Thakur College of Architecture and Planning in 2022. With a deep understanding of design principles and a keen eye for aesthetics, she specializes in creating captivating and functional spaces for her clients. Pari's commitment to excellence and dedication to delivering exceptional results have earned her recognition in the industry. With a focus on creativity and attention to detail, she takes pride in crafting designs that not only reflect her clients' unique styles but also optimize their spaces for maximum functionality. From residential to commercial projects, she brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to each endeavor.
