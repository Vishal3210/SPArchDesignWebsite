BRIEF

About Us

Welcome to S|P Arch and Design, where we believe that great design has the power to transform lives. Our team is passionate about creating beautiful, functional spaces that reflect the unique style and needs of our clients. At S|P Arch and Design, we understand that the process of designing a space can be overwhelming. That's why we work closely with our clients to guide them through every step of the design process, from initial concept to final installation. We are skilled at listening to our clients' needs and desires, and translating them into cohesive, stylish designs. Our design philosophy is rooted in the belief that a space should be both beautiful and functional. We believe that great design is not just about aesthetics, but also about how a space feels and functions. That's why we take a holistic approach to every project, considering everything from traffic flow and lighting to color and texture. We believe that everyone deserves to live in a space that reflects their unique style and enhances their quality of life. That's why we offer a range of design services to meet the needs of our clients, from full-scale interior design to custom furniture and decor.

Principles

At S|P Arch and Design, we specialize in functional, personalized, and beautiful interior design. With client collaboration, we create comfortable and tailored spaces that prioritize every detail. From material selection to furniture placement, our designs achieve balance and visual appeal. Our goal is to enhance the lives of those who inhabit our creations.

Values

Our firm values creativity, quality, collaboration, sustainability, and integrity. We prioritize aesthetics and functionality, collaborating with clients for tailored projects. Sustainability is crucial with an eco-friendly focus. We conduct business with honesty, professionalism, and respect, defining our design philosophy of inspiring and functional spaces.

Vision

Our firm envisions creating transformative spaces that enhance lives through beautiful and functional design. We strive to inspire, comfort, and delight occupants. Staying ahead of design trends, we offer a personalized, collaborative approach. Our goal is industry leadership, known for innovation and dedication to clients' visions.

EXECUTION STEPS

Design Process

01

Initial Client Brief

Site Survey Discussing clients' desires and needs. Ask questions and explore how they envision the look and feel of the finished product.

03

Conceptual Development

Measurements noted, you can start transforming raw ideas into something a little more refined. Finding things which can meet your clients need: -Features, Finishes, Furniture, Colours, mood board, etc.

05

Design Development

After approval of proposal and preliminary design, the sketching stage heads towards design development. Design Development stage includes: -Layout, Elevation -Finishes, Fabrics -Furniture -Colours -Lighting, Decor -Appliances

07

Construction & Inspection

Everything is in order: -Contractors are ready. -Materials are ordered. Main responsibility during the construction & Inspect.

02

Site Survey

Analysis on Clients existing space. Survey is even completed during the initial client briefing. Pointers to be performed during site survey: -Explore every nook and cranny of the space. -Take lots of picture. -Measure everything. -Ask for a copy of as-built drawings.

04

Sketching

Turning ideas into Drawings. Digital Floor plans &3D models. Design with a proposal and a rough estimate.

06

Construction Documentation

Two main stages in Construction Documentation: -Construction Drawing Set (Plans) -Specifications
